Frequently Asked Questions

How does ParkPeep work?

ParkPeep connects to the internet to pull live wait time data directly from Disneyland® and Walt Disney World® with the help of https://queue-times.com/. Just plug it in, connect it to Wi-Fi, select your desired park and you'll start seeing wait times on your screen.

Does it require a subscription?

No, ParkPeep does not require a subscription. Just connect to Wi-Fi and enjoy!

Can I update the firmware?

Yes! You can update the firmware to improve functionality or add new features. Simply connect the device to your computer and use the button below to install the latest update.

Is it compatible with both Disneyland and Disney World?

Yes, ParkPeep provides real-time data from both Disneyland® and Walt Disney World®. You can switch between parks on the main menu. The complete list of parks is Disneyland, California Adventure, Animal Kingdom, Hollywood Studios, EPCOT, and Magic Kingdom.
